# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is a small public library of agent skills and workflow templates.
Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep discussions practical,
respectful, and focused on improving the repository.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Keep feedback technical and specific.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing private data.
- Assume public issues and pull requests are permanent public records.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private contact details, credentials, tokens, or private business
  information.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues or pull requests;
and block users when needed to protect the project and its contributors.

Report conduct concerns to ris@sereja.tech.
